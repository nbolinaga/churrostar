    To inquiry about catering Contact Us.

  • Are your products suitable for vegetarians?

    Yes, all of our products are meat free!

  • How can I provide feedback for an experience I had in a ChurroStar?

    Feedback, good or bad, is always welcomed. To do so please Contact Us.

    We try to have all our products in every ChurroStars but due to space limitations we cannot offer all foods in some of our locations, make sure to visit them all!

    The best way to stay informed about us in through our social media
    ( Facebook - Instagram )

  • What products does Churrostar use that might contain peanut?

    All our prodcuts are free of peanut, we do however use products that contain other types of nuts, for example Nutella.

    Yes, we are open during the pandemic, we respect and follow all practices to prevent the spread of covid-19 and we hope you do too! to check a locations hours simply click the "hours" button below your desired location.
